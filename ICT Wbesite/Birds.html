Birds

What are Birds?

Birds are warm-blooded egg-laying vertebrate distinguished by the possession of feathers wings, and a beak and by being able to fly. This includes parrots, eagles, falcons.

Types of Birds

  • Eagles
  • Parrots
  • Chickens
  • Falcons
  • Ducks

Bird Characteristics

Birds share several characteristics, including:

  • Have feathers on their skin
  • Warm-blooded metabolism
  • Laying eggs
  • Breathe air through lungs
