Animals

What are Animals?

Animals are are living things that can move and eat and react to the world through itd senses, esp. of sight and hearing. They include amphibians, reptiles, birds, fishes, and mammals.

Types of Animals

  • Amphibians
  • Fishes
  • Birds
  • Mammals
  • Reptiles

Mammal Characteristics

Reptiles share several characteristics, including:

  • Motility (Abilty to move)
  • Sexual reproduction
  • Specialized tissues
  • Heterotrophy (Ability to ingest food)
