Reptiles

What are Reptiles?

Reptiles are cold-blooded vertebrates that breathe air and have scaly skin. They include crocodiles, snakes, chameleons, lizards, and more.

Types of Reptiles

  • Snakes
  • Dinosaurs
  • Lizards
  • Crocodiles
  • Chameleons

Reptiles Characteristics

Reptiles share several characteristics, including:

  • Scaly skin
  • Cold-blooded metabolism
  • Laying eggs (most species)
  • Breathe air through lungs
