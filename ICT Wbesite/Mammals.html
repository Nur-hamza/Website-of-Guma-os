Mammals

What are Mammals?

Mammals are warm-blooded vertebrates that breathe air and have furry skin. They include lions, tigers, bears, horses, and more.

Types of Mammals

  • Lions
  • Whales
  • Tigers
  • Dogs
  • Bears

Mammal Characteristics

Reptiles share several characteristics, including:

  • Scaly skin
  • Cold-blooded metabolism
  • Laying eggs (most species)
  • Breathe air through lungs
