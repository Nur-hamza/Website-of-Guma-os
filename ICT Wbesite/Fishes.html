Fishes

What are Fishes?

Fishes are cold-blooded vertebrates that breathe through their gills and have scaly skin. They include sharks, tuna fishes, jawless fishes, catfishes, and more.

Types of Fishes

  • Jawless Fishes
  • Sharks
  • Catfishes
  • Tuna Fishes
  • Koi Fishes

Mammal Characteristics

Reptiles share several characteristics, including:

  • Scaly skin
  • Cold-blooded metabolism
  • Have fins
  • Breathe air through gills
