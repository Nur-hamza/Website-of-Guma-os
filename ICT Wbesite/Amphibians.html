Amphibians

What are Amphibians?

Amphibians are cold-blooded vertebrate animals that is basically able to live both on land and in water. They include frogs, salamanders, and toads.

Types of Amphibians

  • Frogs
  • Salamanders
  • Toads
  • Newts
  • Axolotl

Amphibians Characteristics

Amphibians share several characteristics, including:

  • Smooth skin
  • Cold-blooded metabolism
  • Laying eggs (most species)
  • Breathe air through lungs
